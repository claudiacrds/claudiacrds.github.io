About this Master

The Master in Journalism, Media and Globalisation is a 2-year experience provided by the Erasmus Mundus Program. This master offers a global education that will help students learn how to their journalistic skills with the knowledge acquired. Besides this, they will also learn how to analyse, interpret and generate knowledge about all the global changes that have been challenging the traditional boundaries between media, politics, technology and culture. 

All the students will be given the opportunity to study and live in at least two different European countries, during the master. The first year will be spent between the Aarhus University and the Danish School of Media and Journalism. In the second year, the students should choose between four different specialisations.
For the ones who wish to study in 3 different countries during their master degree, they have the opportunity to spend their second semester at one of these universities: University of California at Berkeley (Berkeley), Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile (Santiago), University of Technology Sydney (Sydney), University of Cape Town (Cape Town), Fudan University (Shanghai), University of Hamburg (Hamburg), Lebanese American University (Beirut).
